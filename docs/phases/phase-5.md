# Phase 5 — Commercial Layer

> Monetization. First-party paid apps and third-party paid apps both work.

**Goal:** Real money flowing. Tenants pay for the shell; first-party paid apps work; third-party publishers get paid.

**Phase 5 Gate:** First paying customers on the shell. First paying installs of paid apps. First revenue-share payouts to third-party publishers.

---

## Workstreams (sketch)

| Code | Workstream | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| **U** | Billing rails | Per-seat for shell; per-app for paid apps; usage metering for usage-priced apps |
| **V** | Revenue share | Shopify-style — 30% → 15% at scale — for third-party paid apps |
| **W** | Enterprise tier | SSO, SCIM, audit log retention, IP allowlist, custom roles, private app registry |
| **X** | Marketplace categories | Curated categories, featured listings, editorial reviews |
| **Y** | App trials | Free trial mechanics; auto-conversion or auto-disable on expiry |
| **Z** | App bundles | "BusinessOS Suite" first-party bundle (CRM + Accounts + Sales) at a discount |

Detail comes when Phase 4 is closed.
